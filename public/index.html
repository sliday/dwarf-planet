Dwarf Land

A living world of AI-powered dwarves. 29 cities across the globe, each with unique cultures and autonomous citizens making decisions in real time.

Click any dwarf to inspect their stats, backstory, and personality. Sponsor their brain to upgrade their AI reasoning — they'll make smarter decisions (for a while).

How Dwarf Land Works

The World

A 500×250 tile planet with continents, oceans, mountains, forests, and deserts. 29 real-world cities with culturally named dwarves. The map wraps horizontally — it's a globe.

AI-Powered Decisions

Dwarves use a 4-tier AI system for decision-making:

  • Simple — basic needs (eat, sleep, mine)
  • Medium — social + planning
  • Complex — strategy + relationships
  • Premium — deep reasoning + personality

By default, dwarves use Simple tier. Sponsoring upgrades their brain.

Sponsorship

Click a dwarf and sponsor their brain. They get a star badge and upgraded AI reasoning for a limited number of decisions:

Crafting

Dwarves collect items from terrain (Water, Earth, Fire, Wind) and combine them at workshops. Any two items can be combined — AI generates new results and caches them forever. 296 seed recipes included.

Trading & Ships

Dwarves from different cities trade when they meet. Smarter dwarves (higher INT) get better deals. Ambitious dwarves build ships and sail across oceans to trade with distant cities.

Infrastructure

Roads connect cities automatically. Dwarves upgrade roads to railroads (3 iron + 2 wood) for ultra-fast travel. Cities auto-expand when population and resources allow.

Resources & Building

Each city has its own resource pool (food, wood, stone, iron, gold, cloth, ale, herbs). Dwarves mine, farm, build, eat, sleep, and socialize autonomously. Use the Mine/Build/Farm/Road buttons to designate areas on the map.

Continuous World

The world is alive while you watch. State saves automatically every 60 seconds. Come back anytime — your dwarves remember everything.
